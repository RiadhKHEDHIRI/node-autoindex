The voidNULL repository is a project to promote Knowledge. voidNULL is heavily
inspired by the french repository (now dead, RIP!) Madchat.org.

For us Madchat.org is a whole genration symbol.
Because we loved it, we wanted to clone it
to continue the march of the ancestors.

For those who don't know Madchat.org, you can learn more on the french wikipedia page,
you can also find some mirrors of it like Madchat.fr or google "madchat mirrors" for more.

voidNULL repository will be build from scratch and will still in perpetual beta stage.
